## GetResponse Module for PrestaShop

> ⚠️ **Important:** This module is now officially distributed via [PrestaShop Addons Marketplace](https://addons.prestashop.com/en/email-marketing-automation/87144-getresponse.html).  
> Please download and install the module from the official source to ensure you receive automatic updates and dedicated support.

Thanks to GetResponse integration with PrestaShop, you can easily create professional email marketing campaigns for your online store and nurture relationships with your customers. Simply import contact lists and use forms for better customer segmentation.

Send personalized, targeted offers to maximize conversions and increase sales.

### 🚀 Getting Started

1. Get the module from [PrestaShop Addons](https://addons.prestashop.com/en/email-marketing-automation/87144-getresponse.html)
2. Upload and install the GetResponse module via your PrestaShop back office
3. Create a new webservice key in PrestaShop
4. Go to Integration Center in GetResponse to connect your PrestaShop site

📖 Detailed setup instructions are available in the [official documentation](https://www.getresponse.com/help/how-can-i-fully-integrate-my-prestashop-with-getresponse.html).

### ✨ Features

Once you connect your PrestaShop with GetResponse, you can use a wide variety of tools to improve your store:

**Contact segmentation**  
Split your customers into groups based on ecommerce data and personal attributes. This way you can send more personalized content and create lasting relationships with them.

**Product import**  
Promote your products via email by adding product boxes synced with your shop. If you edit products in your shop, the integration will update them automatically in GetResponse.

**Marketing automation**  
Create paths you want your customers to follow, depending on their actions, shopping preferences, and ecommerce history. Set it up once and let GetResponse execute it for you.

**Multichannel promotion**  
Promote your PrestaShop store by distributing your communications via email, SMS, web push, paid ads, and popups. All from one GetResponse dashboard.

**Manage unsubscribes**  
GetResponse plugin syncs unsubscribed contacts both ways (a.k.a. two-way sync), so you can be sure to always respect the privacy protection preferences of your customers.
